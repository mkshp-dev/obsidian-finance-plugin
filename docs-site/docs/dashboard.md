# Dashboard

The **Unified Dashboard** is the central hub of the plugin, providing a comprehensive view of your financial health. It is accessible via the ribbon icon (üí∞) or the command "Open Finance Dashboard".

## üìä Overview Tab

The Overview tab gives you a high-level snapshot of your finances.

### Key Metrics
- **Net Worth**: Total Assets minus Total Liabilities.
- **Total Assets**: Sum of all accounts under the configured Assets root.
- **Total Liabilities**: Sum of all accounts under the configured Liabilities root.
- **Monthly Income/Expenses**: Quick summary of the current month's performance.

### Visualizations
- **Monthly Trends**: A bar chart showing Income vs. Expenses over the last 12 months. This helps you track your savings rate and spending habits.
- **Asset Allocation**: A pie/donut chart showing the distribution of your assets across different account groups (e.g., Cash, Investments, Property).

---

## üè¶ Accounts Tab

The Accounts tab allows you to explore your account hierarchy interactively.

### Features
- **Tree View**: Expand and collapse account groups to see the structure of your chart of accounts.
- **Real-time Balances**: See the current balance for every account and group.
- **Drill-down**: Click on any account to see more details or filter transactions for that specific account.
- **Multi-currency**: Displays balances in their respective currencies.

## ‚öñÔ∏è Balance Sheet Tab

The Balance Sheet tab provides a standard financial statement view.

### Sections
1.  **Assets**: What you own (Cash, Bank Accounts, Investments, Property).
2.  **Liabilities**: What you owe (Credit Cards, Loans, Mortgages).
3.  **Equity**: Your net worth (Previous Retained Earnings + Current Net Income).

### Analysis
- **Period Comparisons**: Compare current balances with previous months or years.
- **Structure**: Follows the standard accounting equation: `Assets = Liabilities + Equity`.

## üìù Usage Tips

- **Refresh Data**: The dashboard automatically refreshes when you modify transactions, but you can also force a refresh if you've edited the file externally.
- **Currency Conversion**: The dashboard uses the price database to estimate values in your reporting currency (usually USD or your local currency). Ensure your prices are up-to-date for accurate Net Worth calculations.
